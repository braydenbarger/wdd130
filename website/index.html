Weapons of Mass Determination

An app that weaponizes procrastination to help you be more productive.

About the App

Weapons of Mass Determination takes your procrastination habits and turns them into productivity weapons. The app uses gamified techniques to motivate you, ensuring that your most distracting habits now contribute to your success.

Features

  • Procrastination Battles: Compete with yourself to defeat your distractions.
  • Time Management Tools: Utilize built-in timers and task organizers.
  • Productivity Streaks: Track your progress and stay motivated.
  • Reward System: Earn rewards as you conquer procrastination.
